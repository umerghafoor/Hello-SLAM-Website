## Recursive Bayes Filter

The Recursive Bayes Filter is the probabilistic foundation under both the Kalman and
particle filters. It maintains a **belief** over the state $x_t$ given all measurements
and controls up to time $t$:

$$
\mathrm{bel}(x_t) \;=\; p(x_t \mid z_{1:t},\, u_{1:t}).
$$

We update this belief in two steps each tick:

1. **Prediction** — push the belief forward with the motion model.
2. **Correction** — reweight by the likelihood of the new measurement.
