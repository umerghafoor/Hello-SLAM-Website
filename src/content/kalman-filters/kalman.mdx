## Kalman & Extended Kalman Filters

When the belief is Gaussian and the motion / sensor models are linear, the Bayes filter
collapses into closed-form updates on a mean $\mu$ and covariance $\Sigma$ — the **Kalman
filter**.

For nonlinear systems we linearize via Jacobians at the current estimate, giving the
**Extended Kalman Filter (EKF)**:

$$
\bar\mu_t = g(u_t, \mu_{t-1}), \qquad \bar\Sigma_t = G_t \Sigma_{t-1} G_t^\top + R_t.
$$
