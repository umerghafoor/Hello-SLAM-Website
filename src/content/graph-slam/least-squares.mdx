## Nonlinear Least Squares

Modern SLAM back-ends frame the problem as minimizing a sum of squared residuals:

$$
x^\star = \arg\min_x \sum_i \lVert e_i(x) \rVert^2_{\Omega_i}.
$$

We’ll cover Gauss–Newton, Levenberg–Marquardt, and the structure of the normal equations
$H \Delta x = -b$.
