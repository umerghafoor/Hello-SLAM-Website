## Motion & Sensor Models

A filter needs two probabilistic models:

- a **motion model** $p(x_t \mid x_{t-1}, u_t)$ that says where the robot likely ended up after a control,
- a **sensor model** $p(z_t \mid x_t)$ that says how likely a measurement is from a given state.

We’ll look at the **odometry** and **velocity** motion models, and the **range-bearing**
sensor model used by most landmark-based SLAM systems.
